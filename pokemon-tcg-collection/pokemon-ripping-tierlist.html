  • Chase $ = approximate raw (ungraded) Near-Mint price of each set's most valuable card — a June 2026 snapshot. Prices swing hard (Prismatic's Umbreon alone ranges ~$1,200–1,700; Mega-era chases have corrected 30–50% from launch).
  • ≈ SIR rate = odds of pulling any Special Illustration Rare. S&V runs ~1 in 40; the Mega era is tighter (~1 in 70–100) because it added the Mega Attack Rare and Mega Hyper Rare tiers above it. A specific chase is far rarer — MHRs sit around 1 in 540–1,260 packs.
  • Rarity tags: SIR = Special Illustration Rare · IR = Illustration Rare · BWR = Black & White Rare · MHR = Mega Hyper Rare (gold). Several top chases are IRs, not SIRs — great art beats rarity tier.
  • The two tabs are ranked separately. Mega Evolution is a brand-new series (Sept 2025–present); its sets aren't slotted against S&V.
